Greek-style salad dressing

Prep: 2 min

Cook: 0

Makes about 250ml

Combine oil, vinegar and lemon juice with garlic and oregano to make this easy

dressing – it's not traditional, but we've also added Dijon mustard for a bit of mellow heat.

Ingredients

  • 8 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 6 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • 1 large garlic clove, crushed
  • ¾ tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp Dijon mustard (optional)

Method

STEP 1

Combine all the ingredients in a small bowl or jug, season well and whisk to combine. You can also combine in a jar, screw the lid on and shake well. (The Dijon mustard helps to emulsify the ingredients, but is optional.) Will keep in an airtight container in the fridge for up to five days.
